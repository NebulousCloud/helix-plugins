Combine Assistant

by Aspect™

  • Description
  • Options
  • License

Combine Assistant, or CA for short, originally created by Blt950 for the Clockwork framework, has been recreated for Helix. While not an entirely accurate copy of the original plugin, it adds several useful features for Combine factions. I plan to improve it at some point and add some more things.

This plugin has built-in support for the CTO (Combine Technology Overlay) plugin, and adds a few more features if the plugin is installed.

To use, bind the +CombineAssistant command to a key, and press and hold that key to open the menu.

Players can customize their experience with these personal settings

Automatic Actions Enabled (default: false)

Whether /me's should automatically be sent when specific actions are performed.

Radio Reply: Affirmative (default: 10-4.)

The message to be sent when the player replies affirmative to a radio request.

Radio Reply: Negative (default: 10-2.)

The message to be sent when the player replies negative to a radio request.

Radio Report: Area (default: Reporting 10-20 at %s.)

The message to be sent when the player reports the area they are in.

Radio Report: Citizen Count (default: Reporting 10-91d count at %s.)

The message to be sent when the player reports the number of citizens in the area they are in.

Search Instructions (default: Spread your legs apart and rest your hands down your sides. Don't turn around, and no sudden movements.)

The instructions to show for when the player instructs somebody before a search.

Stunstick Holster Action (default: clips their stunstick onto their belt.)

The action to be performed when the player holsters the stunstick. Leave blank to disable.

Stunstick Knock Action (default: knocks on the door in front of them with their stunstick.)

The action to be performed when the player knocks on a door with the stunstick. Leave blank to disable.

Stunstick Lower Action (default: lowers their stunstick down, holding it by their hips.)

The action to be performed when the player lowers the stunstick. Leave blank to disable.

Stunstick Off Action (default: clicks their stunstick, turning it off. A brief zapping sound is heard as it dies off.)

The action to be performed when the player turns the stunstick off. Leave blank to disable.

Stunstick On Action (default: clicks their stunstick, turning it on. Small sparks fly off as it comes to life, emitting a brief zapping sound.)

The action to be performed when the player turns the stunstick on. Leave blank to disable.

Stunstick Push Action (default: pushes %s with their stunstick.)

The action to be performed when the player pushes someone with the stunstick. Leave blank to disable.

Stunstick Raise Action (default: raises their stunstick up to shoulder level.)

The action to be performed when the player raises the stunstick. Leave blank to disable.

Stunstick Unholster Action (default: unclips their stunstick from their belt, holding it firmly in their hand.)

The action to be performed when the player unholsters the stunstick. Leave blank to disable.

Tie Action (default: takes a hold of a pair of zip-ties and attempts to restrain %s.)

The action to be performed when the player ties someone.

Untie Action (default: reveals their service knife, slicing %s's zip-ties, freeing them.)

The action to be performed when the player unties someone.
